Bridging the gap between biological signals and solid-state silicon.

Project Praseom is developing next-generation wearable bioelectronic interfaces. By integrating advanced electrochemistry with custom analog microelectronics, we are establishing continuous, non-invasive telemetry profiles for critical physiological analytes.

Core Innovation Architecture

01 / Transduction

High-selectivity microneedle skin patches optimized for continuous interstitial fluid (ISF) sampling without blood access.

02 / Mixed-Signal AFE

Custom ultra-low-noise chopper-stabilized instrumentation architectures engineered to suppress baseband 1/f noise and maximize signal-to-noise ratios.

03 / Edge Compute

Bare-metal low-power firmware stack designed for real-time trend analytics, adaptive stabilization, and deterministic execution profiles.
